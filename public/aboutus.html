All About Beermo

The best place on the web to get your household chores, no matter how big or small, completed by a friendly neighborhood pal, and simply pay them with the beer of your choice.

Rules and Regulations

Either post a favor that you want someone else to complete, or peruse the board for a favor that you want to complete. Each favor is either small, medium, or large, and is worth a beer of value 1, 2, or 3, respectively. Once the favor has been completed, the taskor owes the taskee the beer of his or her choice.
